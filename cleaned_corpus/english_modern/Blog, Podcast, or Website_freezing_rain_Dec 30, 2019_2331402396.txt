For those who enjoyed our green Christmas, winter will come roaring back today with freezing rain, ice pellets and even a snowfall warning( https://weather.gc.ca/warnings/report_e.html?qc67#1911592041984294974201912290504ww1070cwul ).
Snow at times will be mixed with ice pellets beginning in the morning, with also a risk of freezing rain.
Snow and ice pellet amounts will range from 5 to 10 cm.
What snow does fall will be blown about by a northeast wind of 30 km/h, gusting to 50.
Environment Canada is calling for a high of minus 2.
Tonight: Snow will continue to fall, accumulating another 10 cm, with the temperature holding steady near minus 2.
