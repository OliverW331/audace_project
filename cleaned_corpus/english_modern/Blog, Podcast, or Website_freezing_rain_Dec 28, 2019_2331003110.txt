We're in that dead zone between Christmas and New Year's, but the news never truly stops. Here's what you might have missed on Friday, Dec. 27, 2019:
Montrealers feel the gravity of the situation as freezing rain ices sidewalks( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/montrealers-feel-the-gravity-of-the-situation-as-freezing-rain-ices-sidewalks )
As Environment Canada on Friday morning extended its freezing rain warnings across eastern Quebec, at least 17 Montreal and Laval residents found themselves seeking help from first responders after slipping on icy sidewalks and secondary streets. The situation improved in the afternoon as temperatures rose above freezing. Full story here( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/montrealers-feel-the-gravity-of-the-situation-as-freezing-rain-ices-sidewalks ).
Good news/bad news day on Canadiens' medical front( https://montrealgazette.com/sports/hockey/nhl/hockey-inside-out/stu-cowan-good-news-bad-news-day-on-canadiens-medical-front )
There was some bad news Friday when head coach Claude Julien announced that Joel Armia is listed as "week-to-week" with a hand injury he suffered against the Jets. Julien added that Paul Byron had a setback in his recovery from knee surgery on Nov. 19 and is listed as week-to-week and that Jonathan Drouin, who had wrist surgery on Nov. 18, might not be back until the end of January. Full story here( https://montrealgazette.com/sports/hockey/nhl/hockey-inside-out/stu-cowan-good-news-bad-news-day-on-canadiens-medical-front ).
Verdun man missing since Monday found safe and sound( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/verdun-man-missing-since-monday-found-safe-and-sound )
Montreal police on Friday evening found a 76-year-old man who had gone missing from his Verdun seniors' residence on Monday. Police located Raymond Hubbard inside a bank on Laird Blvd. in Town of Mount Royal at about 6 p.m. after receiving a 911 call, said Constable Jean-Pierre Brabant, a spokesperson for the Montreal Police Department. Full story here( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/verdun-man-missing-since-monday-found-safe-and-sound ).
