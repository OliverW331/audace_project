Céline Dion at the Bell Centre: 'It feels so good to be home'( https://montrealgazette.com/entertainment/local-arts/celine-dion-at-the-bell-centre-it-feels-so-good-to-be-home )
Pop icon Céline Dion ended her 16-year residency at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas in June. But if there is another city that could host a residency, it is surely Montreal, in Dion's hometown province.
Monday night, Dion - fully recovered from a throat virus that forced her to reschedule the Montreal concerts on her Courage World Tour( https://montrealgazette.com/entertainment/celine-dions-courage-tour-and-album-marks-beautiful-new-chapter ) - headlined the first of six highly anticipated concerts at the Bell Centre.
