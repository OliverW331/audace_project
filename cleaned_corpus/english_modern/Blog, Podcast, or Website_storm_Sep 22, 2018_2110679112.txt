Quebec Premier Philippe Couillard announced Friday night he will suspend his campaign so he can head to Gatineau and visit areas damaged by a severe storm hours earlier.
A tornado damaged cars and buildings in Gatineau and caused severe damage in the eastern Ontario town of Dunrobin on Friday evening( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/multiple-homes-damaged-in-dunrobin-possible-tornado/wcm/2678a04d-42c2-4405-89d2-6612b0477aec ).
Couillard's plan for Saturday had been to go to Sherbrooke and Magog and end the day in Montreal.
