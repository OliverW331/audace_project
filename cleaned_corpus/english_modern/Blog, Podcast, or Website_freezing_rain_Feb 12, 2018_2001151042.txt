A freezing-rain warning( https://weather.gc.ca/warnings/report_e.html?qc67#5511765584395507201802100504ww1070cwul ) is still in effect for the greater Montreal region Sunday night and could lead to slippery roads and sidewalks on Monday morning. Two to five millimetres of freezing rain are expected.
The forecast calls for freezing rain to change to rain showers or snow flurries Sunday evening, ending around midnight. The temperature could rise to 3 C Sunday evening before falling throughout the night.
The forecast for Monday calls for a mix of sunny and cloudy skies with a high of -5.
Gusts reaching 50 kilometres per hour are also expected in some parts of the province.
