Quebec's Ministry of Forests, Wildlife and Parks has lifted a province-wide ban on campfires( https://montrealgazette.com/news/quebec/risk-of-forest-fires-campfires-banned-in-some-parts-of-quebec ).
The ministry said Monday campfires are now allowed across the province, reversing the ban that had been in effect since last Friday. The decision was effective as of 2:30 p.m.
According to the Société de protection des fôrets contre le feu (SOPFEU), Quebec has seen 452 forest fires this year - significantly higher compared to the 10 year average of 316 fires by this time of year.
