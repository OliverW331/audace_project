Highway 15 pileup
Two people were killed during a massive pileup of cars midday on Wednesday. In all, about 200 vehicles were involved in the crash that appeared to have been caused by a sudden blizzard on Highway 15 in La Prairie. Details here( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/multiple-injuries-reported-in-pileup-on-highway-15-in-brossard ).
