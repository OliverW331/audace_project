Environment Canada has issued a special weather statement for the Montreal area( https://weather.gc.ca/warnings/report_e.html?qc67#59036112053569968201907270504ws1070cwul ), warning that humidity will return with a vengeance as of Sunday.
According to the statement, the current heat will persist through Tuesday, with the humidex values peaking near 40 for the next two days.
The warning urges those in the affected areas to "visit and check up on loved ones (neighbours, friends and family), especially those who suffer from a loss of autonomy, from a chronic disease or those who live alone, in order to ensure they are cool and not dehydrated." It also advises they "drink six to eight glasses of water per day" and "limit physical activities."
