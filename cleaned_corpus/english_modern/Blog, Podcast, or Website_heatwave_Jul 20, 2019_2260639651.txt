As if the heat wasn't enough.
In addition to trying to stay cool in the current heat wave( https://montrealgazette.com/news/local-news/heat-warning-issued-for-montreal-area-as-temperature-could-soar-to-45-c ), Montrealers will potentially have to deal with thunder and lightning.
On Saturday afternoon, Environment Canada issued a severe thunderstorm watch for southern Quebec. According to the warning, the thunderstorms are capable of producing strong wind gusts and heavy rain.
The thunderstorm warning covers Montreal and Laval, as well as the Laurentian, Outaouais and Montérégie regions.
