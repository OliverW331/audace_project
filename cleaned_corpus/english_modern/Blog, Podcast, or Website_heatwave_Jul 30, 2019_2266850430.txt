Environment Canada says we can expect a warm and humid air mass to continue over southern Quebec into Tuesday evening, which will cause oppressive heat. The heat wave will produce a high of 29, humidex 37, with a UV index 7 or high. Wind will also be reaching southwest 20-40 km/h, as clouds fill the afternoon with some showers and thunderstorms.
Tonight: There will be showers, a risk of thunderstorms in the evening, with a low of 20.
