At the same time as Quebec struggles to hit its greenhouse-gas emission targets, millions of dollars in environmental funds are sitting dormant in the province's Green Fund. Calling for a major overhaul of the fund that has an accumulated surplus of $949.3 million, the Conseil de gestion du Fonds vert (CGFV), which oversees the operation, says more than half of its programs need to be revised, re-evaluated or just scrapped because they are not helping.
